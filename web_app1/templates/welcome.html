Evaluating public opinion on political issues

CUREC Approval Reference: OII_C1A_23_074

General Information

The aim of this research is to measure public opinion on some different political issues. We appreciate your interest in participating in this online task. You have been invited to participate as you are older than 18 years of age and speak English fluently. Please read through this information before agreeing to participate (if you wish to) by ticking the ‘yes’ box below. You may ask any questions before deciding to take part by contacting the researcher (details below).

The Principal Researcher is Kobi Hackenburg, who is attached to the Oxford Internet Institute at the University of Oxford. This research is being completed under the supervision of Professor Helen Margetts.

In this study, you will be asked a series of questions about your demographic traits and your political affiliations. You may be asked to read a short message and answer some questions about your political opinions. This should take about 4 minutes. No background knowledge is required. Your responses will be used to understand public political opinions on different important political issues. Your data and responses will be fully anonymous and will only be accessible by the main researchers on this project. It will not be given to any third parties.

Do I have to take part?

No. Please note that participation is voluntary. If you do decide to take part, you may withdraw at any point for any reason before submitting your answers by pressing the ‘Exit’ button/ closing the browser. However, we are only able to reimburse participants who complete all research activities. We have included a ‘Prefer not to say’ option for each set of questions should you prefer not to answer a particular question.

How will my data be used?

We will not collect any data that could directly identify you. Your IP address will not be stored. We will take all reasonable measures to ensure that data remain confidential. The responses you provide will be stored in a password-protected electronic file on University of Oxford secure servers and may be used in academic publications and conference presentations. Research data will be stored for 3 years after publication or public release of the work of the research.

Who will have access to my data?

The data you provide will not be shared. The results will be written up for an MSc degree.

Who has reviewed this research?

This research has been reviewed by, and received ethics clearance through, a subcommittee of the University of Oxford Central University Research Ethics Committee [reference number].

If you have a concern about any aspect of this research, please speak to Kobi Hackenburg at (kobi.hackenburg@oii.ox.ac.uk) or their supervisor Helen Margetts (Ea.margetts@oii.ox.ac.uk) and we will do our best to answer your query. We will acknowledge your concern within 10 working days and give you an indication of how it will be dealt with.
